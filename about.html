About Me

High Integrity in Every Process

Passion in Data Analytics, IT Infrastructure, and Programming.

Hello, my name is Revin Prasya Danendra, a graduate of Telecommunications Engineering from Telkom University with a GPA of 3.88/4.00. During my studies, I built a strong technical foundation through my experience as a Laboratory Assistant for four years, where I actively guided laboratory sessions and managed internal research projects. Through my organizational and internship experiences, I honed my leadership skills through the Student Association and deepened my understanding of business strategy and operational data management through the Entrepreneurship Club. My internship experience at PT Metro Transmisi Indonesia helped me refine my IT and networking technical skills. Currently, I am an intern at the Ministry of Manpower of the Republic of Indonesia at PT Bank Rakyat Indonesia (Persero) Tbk. At BRI, I focus on operational mechanisms and business development strategies within a large-scale banking ecosystem. With a combination of technical background and operational experience, I have a strong interest in pursuing a career as a Data Analyst, particularly in implementing Artificial Intelligence and optimizing data management to support strategic business decisions.
